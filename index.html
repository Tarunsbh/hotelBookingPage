Reservation Policy and Terms & Conditions

  • Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability and may be chargeable by the hotel directly.
  • Check-in time is 2:00 PM, Check-out time is 11:00 AM.
  • Guests are required to produce a valid ID proof at the time of check-in.
  • Foreign guests need to produce a valid passport with a visa, whereas Indian guests need to produce a valid government-approved ID. PAN card will not be considered as proof.
  • Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in.
  • All special requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
  • Cancellation Policy:
    • If cancelled before 7 days: 85% refund
    • Between 7 days - 72 hours: 50% refund
    • Less than 72 hours, No-Shows, or Early Checkout: No refund
  • Local citizens of Delhi and Delhi NCR are not eligible for booking rooms.
  • We reserve the right to cancel or modify reservations where it appears that a customer has engaged in fraudulent or inappropriate activity or under other circumstances where it appears that the reservations contain or resulted from a mistake or error.
  • To make modifications or cancellations, please log in to the "Cancel/Reschedule Booking" section on the booking page.
  • Visitors are not allowed in guest rooms.
  • Refunds shall be initiated within 48 hours of receiving the request, and the payment would be credited within 5-7 working days via the same mode as used while making the booking.
